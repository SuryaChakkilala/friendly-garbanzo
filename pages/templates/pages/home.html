About IAQHEI

Quality is doing the right things right and is uniquely defined by each individual. Since quality enhancement is a continuous process, the IQAC becomes a part of the institution's system and works towards realization of the goals of quality enhancement and sustenance. Quality is not what happens when what you do matches your intentions. It is what happens when what you do matches your stakeholders' expectations.
The establishment of Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) by Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF) deemed to be University is a major step in pushing long-term quality standards. IQAC in KLEF is a significant administrative body that is responsible for all quality matters. The prime responsibility of KLEF – IQAC is to initiate, plan and supervise various activities that are necessary to increase the quality of the education in the University. The IQAC of KLEF takes a major role in maintaining quality standards in teaching, learning, evaluation and the research with a defined and achievable outcome. The IQAC at KLEF can promote and determine quality related activities and issues through various programmes and activities such as seminars, workshops, symposia, conferences, panel discussions, role playing exercises, (model) demonstrations, case studies, academic meetings and any such kind of event or programme for all the stakeholders of the University.
As part of that, IQAC at KLEF conducting a conference named “IAQHEI” (Innovative mechanisms & standards for Assuring Quality in Higher Education Institutions) with a galaxy of speakers from national & international institutions and organizations.
The aimed objective of this conference (IAQHEI) is to provide opportunity for the different areas delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences on initiation, improvement, assurance and sustainability of Quality methods in Higher Education. It gives a great platform to the rare communion of experts in prioritizing the rubrics, mechanisms, standards, metrics and key performance criteria to attain the core values of Higher Education and Research. The conference is designed to conduct various result oriented sessions like paper presentations on quality improving methods, metrics etc.
We hope that this conference results constituted significant contribution to the knowledge in improving quality in higher education, clarifications, institutional ranking procedures and outcome based education.

About KL

The KL College of engineering was started in the Academic Year 1980-81 and attained autonomous status in 2006 and conferred with Deemed University (Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation Deemed to be University) status in the Year 2009 under Section 3 of UGC act 1956. NAAC assessed the University and accredited for a period of five years from 2018 to 2023 with CGPA of 3.57 with A++ grade. The University has accorded Category-1 status. Research has taken a quantum step towards quality improvement through publishing in Scopus/SCI Indexed journals building an h-Index of 52 at the moment. KLEF has established an ecosystem to promote innovations including a center for Innovation, Incubation and Entrepreneurship development leading to start-ups in different disciplines.
